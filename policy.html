What is Policy Debate?

The most well-established event of debate, policy debate revolves around a year-long resolution that encompasses a wide diversity of arguments. Under the resolution, or topic, each team presents its own plan to address a current problem in the United States; for 2018-2019, students will discuss immigration. Before tournaments, debaters collaborate to prepare extensive affirmative and negative evidence. Policy teaches skills that go far beyond the realm of debate, such as nuanced argumentation, strong public speaking abilities, and efficient research practices. The team will attend local tournaments, as well as National Circuit invitationals to gain the best all-around knowledge of debate.
